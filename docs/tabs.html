Tabs

A tab bar with switchable panels. Point each tab at a panel with data-tab="#id"; the optional JS activates it and toggles panel visibility. In frameworks, render the active tab + panel from your own state.

CategoryClass / attrEffect

Sizetabs--sm tabs--lgSmall / large
